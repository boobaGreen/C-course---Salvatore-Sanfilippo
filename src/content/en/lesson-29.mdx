## Makefile: The Physics of Time

A `Makefile` is more than a simple script; it's a dependency graph. `make` compares the last modification time (**mtime**) of the source file with that of the binary file.

<Callout type="pro" title="Make's Hardware Logic">
  If `toyfort.c` has a newer timestamp than `toyfort` (the binary), `make`
  launches the compilation command. If the binary is newer, `make` does nothing.
  This saves hours of work in projects with thousands of files.
</Callout>

### Production Optimizations

We use the `-O2` flag. This doesn't just enable mathematical optimizations; it rearranges code to favor the **CPU Pipeline** and **Branch Prediction**.
